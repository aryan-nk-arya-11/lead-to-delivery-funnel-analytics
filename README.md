# 📊 Lead-to-Delivery Funnel Analytics

## 🚀 Overview
This project analyzes the end-to-end Lead-to-Delivery funnel (Lead → Pre-book → Booking → Delivery) to evaluate conversion efficiency, operational performance, and customer experience across multiple stores.

Using a dataset of 1,000 leads, the analysis identifies key drop-offs, bottlenecks, and performance gaps, and provides data-driven recommendations to improve business outcomes.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze funnel conversion at each stage  
- Identify drop-off points and inefficiencies  
- Evaluate delivery turnaround time (TAT)  
- Understand cancellation patterns and customer behavior  
- Provide actionable insights to improve conversion and customer experience  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Total Leads: 1000  
- Multiple store locations  
- Key fields include:
  - Lead stage (Lead, Pre-book, Booking, Delivery)  
  - Dates (Lead, Booking, Delivery)  
  - Cancellation reasons  
  - Customer ratings  
  - Lead categories (Hot, Warm, Cold)  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Conversion Rate (~81.2%)  
- Cancellation Rate (~27.1%)  
- Average TAT (10–11 days)  
- Customer Rating (~4.9/10)  

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Funnel Analysis (stage-wise conversion tracking)  
- TAT (Turnaround Time) Analysis  
- Cancellation Root Cause Analysis  
- Customer Segmentation (Hot/Warm/Cold leads)  
- Customer Satisfaction Analysis (Ratings/NPS)  

---

## 📉 Key Insights
- Strong conversion after booking, but significant drop-offs in early stages  
- High cancellation rate driven by pricing and financing issues  
- Warm leads show high potential but lack effective follow-ups  
- Delivery TAT slightly higher than industry benchmarks  
- Customer satisfaction is relatively low  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Improve follow-up strategies for warm leads  
- Introduce flexible financing options  
- Optimize delivery processes to reduce TAT  
- Enhance customer communication and transparency  
- Strengthen post-delivery engagement
